About

Nothing much to see here. Just me awkwardly trying to describe myself.

Hi everyone! My name is Janette Campbell, and I’m passionate about making clothes that fit the end user. I got my start in technical design at Catherines, where I learned what it took to be a technical designer. My background in patternmaking came in handy quite a bit. I also had the privilege of working with some great people who made the work not seem like work.

You can find me trying to learn something new when I'm not working on tech packs. My hobbies include woodworking, sewing, and cooking.

If you like my work, please contact me. I can't wait to learn more about your brand's design needs.
